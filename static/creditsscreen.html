Help

  • Homepage

    Courses you ahve added are listed at the top of the page in a tab view. Click on courses to view listings of studetns who have current study sessions for that course.

    Adding a course

    To add a new course, navigate to "My Courses". Once in the "My Courses" page, you can delete courses by clicking the red "X" or add courses by clicking th plus sign in the top right corner of the page. Once you have added a course, lisitings of other students' study sessions for that course will appear on the homepge. You can also add you own study session for that course for other students to see.

  • Sessions

    To add a session, navigate to the "My Session". You can publish where and when you will be studying so that others can find you.

  • Maps

    Click on the map button to see your location as well as the location of others studying around you.
