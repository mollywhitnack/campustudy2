Help

  • Adding a course

    To add a new course, click on the book logo in the top right corner. Once you hace added a course you can see other students who are studying for that course or create a study session.

  • Sessions

    To add a session click on the "My Sessions" bar at the bottom of the page. You can publish where and when you will be studying so that others can find you.

  • Maps

    Click on the map button to see your location as well as the location of others studying around you.
